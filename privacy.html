Privacy Policy

Last updated 7 July 2026

Elis Spa ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our website and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you contact us through WhatsApp or the request form, you may share your name, phone number and preferred treatment. We use these details only to respond to your enquiry and arrange your appointment.

How we use it

Your details are used to confirm bookings and provide our services. We do not sell your personal information. We may keep booking records to serve you better on future visits.

Cookies and analytics

Besides the essential cookies needed for the site to function, we use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how the site is used. Analytics cookies are only set after you accept them in the cookie banner; we use Google Consent Mode so they stay disabled by default. See our Cookie Policy for details.
